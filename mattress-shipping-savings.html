Frequently Asked Questions

Is the large package surcharge actually negotiable?

Yes — it's one of the most negotiable categories in a carrier contract for mattress shippers. Most brands have never asked because their carrier rep has no incentive to bring it up. We have pre-negotiated rates in this category specifically.

Our carrier gave us a discount — why would there still be room?

Because the discount applies to base rates, not surcharges. Large package, additional handling, and residential delivery premiums are separate line items — often set at default carrier rates regardless of volume.

Will this disrupt our current operations?

No. In most cases, the only change is a carrier transition (UPS to FedEx or vice versa). We manage that process completely. Your fulfillment team keeps doing exactly what they're doing.

How fast can we expect to see impact?

Initial savings from pre-negotiated rate categories can hit within days of implementation. For a company spending $300K/year on shipping, that's often $45K–$75K returned to margin annually.
