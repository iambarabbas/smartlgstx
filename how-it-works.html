Frequently Asked Questions

How long until I see savings?

Initial savings using pre-negotiated rates can be in place in as little as 1 week. Full optimization across all 250+ contract line items takes 3–6 months.

What's your fee structure?

Gain-share only. We take a percentage of the savings we recover, billed quarterly. If we don't save you money, you owe us nothing.

What about my current shipping software?

We offer a white-labeled shipping platform free to all clients. It integrates with most major eCommerce and WMS platforms. If you need to stay on your current software, we can still work with you.

What size company do you work with?

We focus on companies spending $100K–$500K per year on UPS and/or FedEx. Most clients have $5M–$30M in annual revenue and ship heavy, bulky, or oversized products.

Can you help reduce UPS shipping costs for my business?

Yes. We specialize in negotiating UPS contracts for mid-sized shippers. Our founder spent 15 years at UPS and knows exactly which line items have room to move — and which ones your carrier rep hopes you never ask about.

What are the most common hidden shipping charges?

Large package surcharges, additional handling fees, dimensional weight adjustments, residential delivery premiums, and fuel surcharges. These surcharges often make up 30–50% of your total shipping cost and are rarely negotiated.

How do I know if I'm overpaying for shipping?

If you've never had a carrier contract audit, you're almost certainly overpaying. Even companies that have "negotiated" their rates typically leave 10–20% on the table because they only negotiate base rates — not the 250+ other line items.
