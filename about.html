The Person Negotiating For You Used to Be the Person Negotiating Against You.

15+ years on the carrier side. Now working from the other side of the table.

Chris Tweedy — Founder & CEO

Chris Tweedy spent 15+ years at one of the nation's largest carriers in sales and customer solutions. During that time, he sat across the table from thousands of shipping negotiations — on the carrier's side.

He knew which line items companies fought for. He knew which concessions the carrier was willing to make — and which ones they'd never offer unless pushed. He knew which surcharge categories had real flexibility, and which carrier reps were bluffing.

He also knew that most businesses — even large, sophisticated ones — left significant money on the table in every negotiation. Not because they weren't smart. Because they didn't have someone who'd seen both sides.

After leaving the carrier, Chris spent time as a shipping executive at a growth-stage company. Sitting on the buyer's side for the first time, he watched his own carrier rep use every tactic he'd once relied on.

He launched Smart LGSTX with a simple thesis: companies deserve an expert who knows how carriers actually think.
