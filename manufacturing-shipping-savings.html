Frequently Asked Questions

How does Smart LGSTX get paid?

Gain-share only — we take a percentage of the savings we recover, billed quarterly. If we don't save you money, you owe us nothing.

Our carrier gave us a discount — why would there still be room?

Because the discount applies to base rates, not surcharges. Large package, additional handling, and delivery premiums are separate line items — often set at default rates regardless of volume.

Will this disrupt our current operations?

No. In most cases, the only change is a carrier transition (UPS to FedEx or vice versa). We manage that process completely.

How fast can we expect to see impact?

Initial savings from pre-negotiated rate categories can hit within days of implementation. Full optimization typically takes 3–6 months.
